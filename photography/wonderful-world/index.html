The world is always wonderful one when we see it carefully. In the early 2020, when everything went slowly, I made a detailed survey about things around me. But this time, I wanted to take a different approach from the previous project a street photography one. I still used a Leica but the Leica sofort. It is an Instax camera which has a very limited number of functions. I put myself under lots of constraints. By point-and-shoot, I was wondering where I would be led to and when stopped and looked back, how wonderful the journey would be. The project turned out very pleasantly. I took around 30 boxes Instax films and selected a few of them. These photos are organized into three chapters, color, texture and solitude. The theme of the album is obvious to me and I hope it can obvious to you. At the end of day, the simplicity of these photos all raise the same tone. The world is wonderful.
